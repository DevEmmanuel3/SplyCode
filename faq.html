Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Last Updated: February 1, 2025

1. Do I need to create an account to access courses on SplyCode?

No, you do not need to create an account to browse or access courses on SplyCode. You can start learning right away without the need for account registration. However, we may collect basic usage data to improve our services.

2. How can I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply visit the course page, select the course you’re interested in.90% of the courses are free

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We use paystack to make our payments

4. Are the courses free?

Some of our courses are free, while others require payment. Each course page will clearly indicate whether the course is free or paid. We also offer special discounts and promotions from time to time.



5. Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied with the course?

We offer refunds under specific conditions. Please refer to our Refund Policy for more details. Generally, refund requests must be made within a specified period after purchase, and the course must not have been completed to be eligible for a refund.

6. Can I download the course materials?

Yes Most of the course materials, such as videos and documents, can be accessed and viewed online. However, downloading or distributing the materials is prohibited unless explicitly stated otherwise on the course page.

7. How do I track my progress in a course?

Although SplyCode does not require an account, we do offer a simple method to track your progress. You can manually note which lessons you have completed or use the built-in progress tracker for some courses where available. You can always return to any lesson or module at any time.

8. Can I access the courses on mobile devices?

Yes! SplyCode is fully optimized for mobile devices, so you can access and take courses directly from your smartphone or tablet. Just visit the website through your mobile browser and start learning on the go!

9. Do you offer certificates for course completion?

Currently, we do not offer certificates for course completion. However, you will have access to all course materials and can reference your progress. We are considering adding certificates in the future, so stay tuned!



10. Can I contact the instructors if I have questions?

Yes,While we do not provide direct access to instructors, you can use the course forums or comment sections (where available) to ask questions and engage with other learners. We encourage a collaborative learning environment!

11. How do I get support if I encounter issues with the website or courses?

If you have any technical issues or questions about the courses, you can reach our support team by emailing us at splycode@gmail.com. We will respond to your inquiry as quickly as possible.

12. Can I suggest a new course for SplyCode?

Yes, we welcome suggestions! If you have an idea for a course that you would like to see on SplyCode, feel free to reach out to us at splycode@gmail.com. We love hearing from our users and are always looking for new content ideas.

13. Will my data be safe on SplyCode?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. While you do not need to create an account, we do collect basic usage data (such as IP addresses) to improve the website and our services. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details on how we protect your data.

14. How do I stay updated on new courses and promotions?

You can stay updated by visiting our website regularly.

If you have any additional questions, feel free to reach out to us at splycode@gmail.com.
