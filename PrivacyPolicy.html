Splycode - Privacy Policy

Effective Date:February 1, 2025

At Splycode ("We," "Us," or "Our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit and use our online course website (“Website”). By accessing or using our Website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Since Splycode does not require account creation, we do not store personal information such as usernames or passwords. However, we may collect the following information:

  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you make a purchase or sign up for course-related communication.
  • Payment Information: When purchasing courses, payment details such as credit card information or other payment method details may be processed through third-party payment processors.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with our Website, such as pages viewed, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process your course purchases and provide you with access to purchased courses.
  • Communicate with you regarding your course purchases, updates, and promotions (if you opt-in).
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of our Website.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. We use industry-standard encryption for payment transactions and secure servers to store your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors that help us process payments, provide course materials, or improve Website functionality. These third-party providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only as necessary to provide services to Splycode.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Website and store certain information about your preferences. Cookies help us analyze trends, improve user experience, and deliver more personalized content. You can control cookie settings through your browser, but note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Website.

6. Your Rights and Choices

As a user of Splycode, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request to view the personal information we have collected from you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (such as when we are required to retain your information for legal purposes).
  • Opt-Out of Communications: You can opt-out of receiving promotional or marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send you.

7. Third-Party Links

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information to them.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates. By continuing to use the Website after changes are made, you consent to the updated terms.
