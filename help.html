Help Center | SplyCode

Last Updated: February 1, 2025

Welcome to SplyCode Help Center!

If you're having trouble or have any questions about using SplyCode, you're in the right place! Below you'll find answers to some common questions and tips to help you make the most of our online courses. If you need additional assistance, please don't hesitate to contact us.

1. How do I start a course on SplyCode?

Getting started is easy! No account creation is needed. Simply follow these steps:

  • Browse our courses on the homepage or through the course catalog.
  • Choose the course you want to learn.
  • If it's a paid course, click the "Enroll" button, proceed with payment, and you'll get immediate access to the course content.
  • If the course is free, simply click on the "Start Learning" button, and you'll be able to access the content right away.

Once you've enrolled, you can start learning at your own pace!



2. Do I need to create an account?

No, you don't need to create an account to access or take courses on SplyCode. You can start learning instantly without the hassle of account registration. However, if you want to track your progress, we recommend saving the course link or bookmarking it for easy access.

3. How do I pay for a course?

To pay for a course, you can select a course and proceed to checkout. We accept a variety of payment methods, including:

  • Paystack

Once payment is confirmed, you will instantly get access to the course. You can start learning immediately after the transaction is processed.

4. Can I access the course on my mobile device?

Yes! SplyCode is fully optimized for mobile devices, so you can access and take courses on your smartphone or tablet. All course materials can be viewed easily on any mobile browser, making it convenient to learn on the go.

5. How do I contact support if I have issues?

If you're experiencing any technical issues or have specific questions, our support team is here to help. You can reach us by:

  • Sending an email to splycode@gmail.com.
  • For urgent inquiries, please include a detailed description of the issue you're facing, including any error messages or screenshots if possible.

We aim to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible, usually within 24–48 hours during business days.



6. How do I track my course progress?

While SplyCode doesn’t require an account, we suggest manually tracking your progress. You can easily note which lessons or modules you’ve completed. You can revisit any course at any time by saving the course page or bookmarking it for easy access. This way, you can pick up where you left off.

7. Can I download the course content?

Currently, SplyCode does not offer the ability to download course materials (videos, documents, etc.). All content must be accessed online via the website. However, you can revisit and view the materials at any time as long as the course is available.

8. Are the courses self-paced?

Yes! All courses on SplyCode are self-paced. You can start, pause, and complete the courses at your own convenience. There are no deadlines, so you can learn at a pace that works best for you.

9. Can I access the course after it expires?

Once you've enrolled in a course, you'll have lifetime access to the course content. Even if the course expires or is removed from the platform, you can still access the materials you've already viewed. We recommend saving any important course materials to your device for future reference.

10. How do I stay updated with new courses?

check our website regularly for updates on new content and upcoming courses.

11. Do you offer certifications for course completion?

At this time, SplyCode does not offer certificates of completion for our courses. However, we’re continually improving our offerings, and this may be a feature added in the future. Stay tuned for updates!

If you still have questions or need further assistance, don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help!
