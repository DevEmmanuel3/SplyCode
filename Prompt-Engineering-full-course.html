Prompt Engineering Full Course


Course outline

Prompt engineering is one of the newest and most in-demand skills in tech. In this course, you’ll learn how to write powerful prompts to get high-quality results from tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more. We’ll teach you how to think like an AI - how to structure questions, give instructions, and even design AI workflows. Whether you’re a student, developer, writer, or entrepreneur, this course will teach you how to make AI tools work smarter for you. No coding required - just curiosity and creativity!
