Cookies Policy for SplyCode

Last Updated: February 1, 2025

At SplyCode, we use cookies to enhance your experience while browsing our website. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. Below is an explanation of how we use cookies and your options to manage them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. These files are used to remember your preferences and actions during and after your visit, so you don’t have to re-enter information each time you return to the website. Cookies help improve your experience by making it more efficient and personalized.

How We Use Cookies

SplyCode uses cookies for several purposes, including:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of the website, such as navigation and access to secure areas. Without these cookies, certain features cannot be provided.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website by collecting information on pages visited, time spent on the site, and any error messages encountered. This allows us to improve our website’s performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow the website to remember your preferences (such as language or region) and personalize your experience, making it more tailored to your needs.
  • Advertising Cookies: We may use these cookies to track your browsing habits and show you relevant ads on third-party websites. These cookies help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

Types of Cookies We Use

The types of cookies used on SplyCode include:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies are temporary and are deleted once you close your browser. They are used to track your actions within a single session and ensure that the website functions correctly during your visit.
  • Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until you delete them. They are used to remember your preferences or login information for future visits to the website.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but please note that if you do this, some features of SplyCode may not work properly.

To manage cookies, you can follow the instructions provided by your browser’s help menu. Below are links to the cookie management pages for the most popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Safari

These third parties are responsible for their own cookie practices, and we recommend reviewing their privacy and cookie policies for more information.
